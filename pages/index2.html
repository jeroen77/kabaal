Brings beats to the world

KABAAL is an independent, electronic music- and event platform for Amsterdam, Berlin, London, Ibiza, Stockholm and Miami

We will shake up the electronic music industry by walking a different path. KABAAL will not choose for mainstream, but rather upstream. KABAAL believes that the majority of the press covers only the popular side of electronic music for the masses, while the most innovative underground producers are currently releasing the stuff that will become mainstream in the future. These producers need the credits they deserve and KABAAL will promote them and the events they play on.
